About Langton's Ant

Langton's Ant is a mathematical simulation created by Chris Langton in the 1980s. It involves a simple agent ("ant") that moves around a grid based on a set of rules. The ant flips the color of each square it visits, then turns either left or right depending on the color. The simulation begins with a seemingly random pattern, but after some time, the ant eventually starts building a repeating "highway."
